Privacy Policy

Effective Date: February 2026

RedRight Labs LLC (“RedRight,” “we,” “our,” or “us”) provides operational communication and workflow support through the RedRight Isla system. This Privacy Policy explains what information we collect, how we use it, and how it is handled.

1. Information We Collect

We may collect information such as:

  • Name
  • Phone number
  • Email address (if provided)
  • Business and appointment details
  • Message content submitted via SMS or voice
  • Operational scheduling data and client communications

2. How We Use Information

We use information to:

  • Facilitate scheduling and operational communications
  • Send confirmations, reminders, and follow-ups
  • Route messages and booking requests appropriately
  • Support shop operations and coordination
  • Maintain system reliability and security

We do not sell personal information.

3. SMS & Communication Disclosure

RedRight Isla may send operational messages via SMS or voice on behalf of businesses using the system. Message frequency varies based on business activity. Message and data rates may apply.

Consent to receive communications is obtained by the business using Isla. Individuals may opt out of SMS communications by replying STOP where applicable.

4. Third-Party Services

We use third-party service providers to operate our platform, including providers for voice and SMS delivery, hosting, payment processing, workflow automation, and data storage. These providers process information solely to provide services on our behalf.

RedRight processes information as a service provider to businesses using Isla and does not independently determine the purpose of client communications.

5. Data Retention

We retain information only as long as necessary for operational, contractual, or legal purposes. Businesses using Isla are responsible for complying with their own industry-specific retention requirements.

6. Data Security

We implement reasonable administrative and technical safeguards designed to protect information. However, no system can be guaranteed to be 100% secure.

7. Your Rights

You may request access to, correction of, or deletion of personal information by contacting: support@redrightlabs.com

8. Updates

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. The most current version will always be posted on this page.
